============================================

Status
------
IMPLEMENTED

Purpose
-------
Continue from the completed Phase 120 running-system support statement by
publishing one explicit kernel image-contract hardening step over the same

  explicit proof-owned contract step.
- Without this step, later work could blur the admitted kernel image shape
  into broader loader policy, target-family growth, or compiler expansion.
- The repository needed one small phase that made the image contract honest
  without reopening sema, MIR, backend, ABI, or `hal` work.

- Reuse the landed Phase 117 through Phase 120 artifact truth rather than

- Publish the admitted image contract explicitly and keep all remaining
  non-goals explicit.
- Do not widen into dynamic loading, a service manager, broader target-family
  admission, or a broader freestanding workflow statement.
