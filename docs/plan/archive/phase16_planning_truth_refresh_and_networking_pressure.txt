Phase 16 finding
----------------

  so the repository-owned proof forces deterministic multi-step flushes with an
  explicit per-iteration write budget rather than claiming that kernel-driven
  short writes are already part of the admitted runtime contract

  offset and `io.poller_set(...)` transitions are repository truth today, and

  real short-write behavior more directly

  runtime semantics, that tightening belongs to the Phase 12 `io` and hosted
  runtime owners rather than to example-local helpers
