--------------------
- The speculative Canopus kernel roadmap names a real log-service handshake
  as the next bounded service-policy owner after the landed bootstrap-
  capability and critique-hardening work.
- The repository already had the necessary ingredients spread across the
  owned kernel lane: one explicit init-visible endpoint handle, one real send
  and receive syscall path, one consumed-on-success program-capability spawn
  path, and one explicit wait-handle reap path.
- Without this step, later service phases could overclaim that the repository
  already preserves init-owned service policy across real kernel IPC when the
  landed proofs still stopped at mechanism plus hardening.
