Problem being solved
--------------------
- The repository still has more than one credible deferred frontier outside the
  compiler-owned generic-call case, but they belong to different owners and
  should not be pursued together speculatively.
- Once Phase 40 identifies a winning pressure artifact, the repository needs a
  bounded follow-through that respects that owner boundary and keeps the
  non-winning frontier deferred.
- This phase therefore exists as a conditional branch point rather than as one
  guaranteed implementation shape.

Core rule
---------
- Choose one owner only: sema-plus-`.mci` structural constants or
  tooling-plus-linker broader artifact behavior.
- Fix the chosen distortion at its correct owner boundary.
- Keep the non-chosen frontier explicitly deferred.
- Use the admitted pressure artifact from Phase 40 as the main proof surface.
- Avoid widening unrelated language, stdlib, or runtime families.
