1. Capability System Under Real Pressure

	•	revocation
	•	invalidation at scale
	•	long-lived authority graphs

Examples:

Phase 124 — Multi-hop Delegation Chains

Phase 125 — Revocation or Invalidation Model

	•	long-lived vs short-lived caps

	•	leaks
	•	dangling references
	•	invalid access paths

This is where most capability systems fail—not at “send/recv,” but at lifetime complexity.

2. IPC Model Under Load (without optimizing it)

	•	stressed

Possible milestone band

3. Failure as a First-Class Reality

Possible milestone band

Why this matters

Possible milestone band

Phase 136 — Device Failure Path

	•	ensure:
	•	no kernel-level policy explosion

Why this matters

This is where most “microkernels” fail and re-centralize.
